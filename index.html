Our Story

Founded in 2005, RestaurantSphere has been serving exceptional cuisine in a sophisticated atmosphere for over 15 years. Our passion for culinary excellence drives us to create memorable dining experiences.

Our executive chef, Alexander Monet, brings international expertise from working in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe and Asia. We source only the freshest local ingredients to create dishes that celebrate both tradition and innovation.

At RestaurantSphere, we believe that dining is an art form. From our carefully curated wine list to our impeccable service, every detail is designed to provide an unforgettable experience.
